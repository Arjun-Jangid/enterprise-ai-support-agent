Employee Handbook

Company Overview

Acme Technologies was founded in 2015 with the mission of helping organizations automate business processes through artificial intelligence and cloud computing. The company serves customers across healthcare, finance, education, retail, and manufacturing industries. The headquarters is located in Bangalore, India, with additional offices in Mumbai, Delhi, Singapore, and London.

The company’s core values are innovation, customer success, transparency, accountability, and continuous learning. Employees are encouraged to experiment with new ideas and collaborate across teams.

Working Hours

Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are expected to work a total of 40 hours per week. Flexible work schedules may be approved by managers based on business requirements.

Employees working remotely must be available during core collaboration hours between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

Attendance Policy

Employees must record attendance through the company portal. Repeated late arrivals may result in discussions with managers and performance improvement plans.

Employees unable to attend work due to illness or emergencies should notify their manager as soon as possible.

Annual Leave

Full-time employees receive 24 days of paid annual leave each year. Unused leave may be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days into the next calendar year.

Leave requests should be submitted at least one week before the planned absence. Managers are responsible for approving leave requests while ensuring business continuity.

Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate is required for illnesses lasting more than two consecutive working days.

Long-term medical leave requests are reviewed by the Human Resources department on a case-by-case basis.

Parental Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to parental leave according to local labor laws. Additional benefits may be provided depending on employment contracts and regional regulations.

Employees planning parental leave should inform Human Resources at least two months in advance whenever possible.

Remote Work Policy

The company supports hybrid work arrangements. Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.

Remote employees must maintain a stable internet connection, attend required meetings, and ensure a productive work environment.

Information Security

All employees are responsible for protecting company information and customer data.

Passwords must not be shared with colleagues or external parties. Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all company systems.

Sensitive information should only be stored in approved company platforms. Personal cloud storage services may not be used for confidential business data.

Acceptable Use of Technology

Company laptops, software, and network resources must be used primarily for business purposes.

Employees must not install unauthorized software or disable security controls. Suspicious emails or cybersecurity incidents should be reported immediately to the Information Security team.

Travel Policy

Business travel must be approved in advance by the employee’s manager.

Reasonable expenses for flights, accommodation, meals, and transportation are reimbursable. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days after travel completion.

Luxury expenses that are not business-related are not eligible for reimbursement.

Expense Reimbursement

Expense claims should be submitted through the finance portal.

Approved reimbursements are generally processed within 10 business days. Claims submitted without supporting documentation may be rejected.

Performance Management

Employee performance reviews are conducted twice each year in June and December.

Performance evaluations consider goal achievement, collaboration, innovation, communication, and professional development.

Employees are encouraged to maintain regular discussions with managers regarding career growth and development opportunities.

Learning and Development

The company provides annual learning budgets to support employee growth.

Employees may attend training programs, technical certifications, conferences, workshops, and online courses relevant to their roles.

Manager approval may be required for high-cost learning programs.

Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism.

Harassment, discrimination, retaliation, and inappropriate workplace behavior are strictly prohibited.

Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action, including termination of employment.

IT Support

The IT Help Desk assists employees with hardware, software, network access, email configuration, VPN connectivity, and security-related issues.

Support requests can be submitted through the internal ticketing system. Critical incidents are prioritized and addressed as quickly as possible.

Human Resources

The Human Resources department manages recruitment, onboarding, benefits administration, employee relations, compensation, and workplace policies.

Employees may contact HR for questions regarding leave balances, benefits, payroll concerns, workplace grievances, and policy clarification.

Benefits

Eligible employees may receive health insurance, life insurance, retirement benefits, wellness programs, and employee assistance services.

Benefit availability may vary depending on employment location and local regulations.

Termination of Employment

Employees wishing to resign must provide written notice according to their employment agreement.

The company reserves the right to terminate employment for performance issues, misconduct, policy violations, or business requirements consistent with applicable laws.

Upon separation, employees must return all company-owned equipment and confidential materials.

Data Privacy

The company collects and processes employee information only for legitimate business purposes.

Personal information is handled according to applicable privacy regulations and internal security standards.

Employees may request information regarding the processing of their personal data through Human Resources.

Emergency Procedures

Employees should familiarize themselves with workplace emergency procedures.

Emergency exits, evacuation routes, and assembly points are communicated during onboarding and displayed within company facilities.

Any workplace accident or safety concern should be reported immediately to management.
